# Reference Output: Generated Personas

*This is the actual output from running the [persona creation prompt](../prompts/strategic-prompts.md#step-2--gemini-persona-creation-prompt) in Gemini, included as a worked example to compare your own run against. These personas were generated with the Step 1 NotebookLM findings on CRED's behavioral levers — the **Habit Loop** and **Gamification** — carried forward as context, and are the same three personas used in the [focus-group stress-test](focus-group-transcript.md).*

---

## Persona 1: Adit — the "Top 1%" High-Net-Worth Professional

**Age:** 38 · **Role:** Executive

**Profile:** Values exclusivity and "irreversibility" in his tools. Uses CRED because it makes him feel part of an elite, "fenced community" of the richest 1% in India.

**Core driver:** Exclusivity & security — status is tied to being inside a closed, high-trust ecosystem, not just to the financial upside.

---

## Persona 2: Riya — the "Aspirational Gamification" Enthusiast

**Age:** 27 · **Role:** Marketing professional

**Profile:** Loves "Spin-the-Wheel," "Jackpot Drops," and earning "gems." Highly motivated by the variable rewards and dopamine spikes found in gamified fintech apps.

**Core driver:** Dopamine & status — engagement is driven by the game-like feel of the reward loop and the ability to show off progress socially.

---

## Persona 3: Vikram — the "Skeptical Utility" Optimizer

**Age:** 45 · **Role:** Business owner (uses the WhatsApp Business API for his own company)

**Profile:** Wary of "marketing blasts" and spam. Values transparency and hates unnecessary steps to redeem a reward.

**Core driver:** Transparency & utility — trust has to be earned through a visible, low-friction mechanism, not assumed.
